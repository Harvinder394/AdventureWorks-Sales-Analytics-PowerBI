# 📊 AdventureWorks Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Sales and Customer Analytics dashboard built using Power BI, following the DMAIC (Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve) methodology.
The objective is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and product contribution to derive actionable business insights.

## 📌 Key Insights
* Revenue is highly concentrated in the Bikes category
* Strong dependency on returning customers (~97%)
* Accessories show high sales volume but low revenue
* Revenue is concentrated in North America and Europe
* Top customers contribute significantly to total revenue

## 💡 Business Recommendations
* Improve new customer acquisition strategies
* Introduce bundling strategies to increase revenue
* Optimize pricing for Accessories
* Expand into new geographic markets
* Reduce dependency on top customers
